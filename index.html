Welcome

I am Associate Professor at the Department of Computer-Science and Software Engineering, Canada Research Chair (Tier II) on Big Data Infrastructures for Neuroinformatics, head of the Big Data Infrastructures for Neuroinformatics lab, and member of the PERFORM centre and Data Science Research Centre at Concordia University in Montreal. Before, I was Researcher at the French National Center for Scientific Research.

My research goal is to build platforms for the efficient and reproducible processing of Big Data. The main applications of my work are in medical image analysis, in particular neuroimaging.
